Our promise

Every child, at their own pace.

Our mission has fit on one page since 1984, and it still does: give children a safe, nurturing, genuinely engaging place to grow — and stand beside the family raising them.

Every child develops at their own rate. Our job is to know your child well enough to see that pace, cheer it on, and have them walking into kindergarten ready — confident, curious, and waving goodbye without looking back. (You're allowed to look back. Most parents do.)

Programs

A classroom for every age and stage.

From first naps to pre-K, one warm hand-off at a time.

Infants

6 weeks – 12 months

Gentle days on your baby's schedule — naps, snuggles, and caregivers who learn every cue.

Waddlers

12 – 18 months

For the almost-walkers: soft places to practice and proud applause for every wobbly step.

Toddlers

18 – 24 months

Big feelings and busy hands — stacking, splashing, and the daily joy of “I did it myself.”

Twos

2 – 3 years

Words arrive by the dozen this year. We keep up — with songs, stories, and patient listening.

Threes

3 – 4 years

Friendship season: sharing, pretend play, and learning to be good company at the sand table.

Preschool

3 – 4 years

Letters, numbers, and questions all day long. Here, curiosity is the curriculum.

Pre-K Readiness

4 – 5 years

Everything kindergarten will ask of them, practiced gently until it feels easy.

Outdoor classrooms

Some lessons need sky.

Gardens to water, worms to meet, mud pies to perfect. Our outdoor classrooms get the same care and intention as the indoor ones — because a child who spends the morning digging comes inside ready to listen, wonder, and nap like a champion.
